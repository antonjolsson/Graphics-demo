Copyright and attribution requirement

OpenTopoMap and OpenStreetMap
For an electronic 3D map with a map layer based on OpenTopoMap / OpenStreetMap data, 
the credit should appear in the corner of the map. For example : © OSM Contributors / OpenTopoMap

MapTiler
For an electronic 3D map with satellite texture layer based on MapTiler data, 
the credit should appear in the corner of the map. For example: © MapTiler

Print
For printed maps, images or video recording a similar attribution should be made in a 
clearly visible textual description near the map, in the same fashion as if you cite a photograph.
